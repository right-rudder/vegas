CSN’s Aviation Technology Professional Pilot program provides an Associate of Applied Science (AAS) degree, blending comprehensive coursework with Vegas Aviation’s specialized flight training. Credits from this program can also be applied toward a Bachelor of Science in Professional Flight at Purdue University Global, creating a seamless pathway for career advancement.
