# Announcing New Ground School Sessions Starting September 9, 2024

Following the success of our recent ground school program, Vegas Aviation is thrilled to announce new sessions beginning this September! Our updated curriculum includes additional topics and a night schedule to better accommodate those with daytime commitments. Price: $300 for all 8 classes.

![Ground school informational banner focused on Instrument Rating with a bullet point list of the schedule](/blog/2024-aug-02-ground-school-banner-1.jpg)
![Ground school informational banner with overall information of what's new, what are the prices and what is the schedule](/blog/2024-aug-02-ground-school-banner-2.jpg)
![Ground school informational banner focused on Private Pilot with a bullet point list of the schedule](/blog/2024-aug-02-ground-school-banner-3.jpg)

In addition to traditional Ground School, our new Instrument Ground School is specifically designed for newly licensed Private Pilots or anyone looking for a refresher course. Whether you’re a current Vegas Aviation student or not, you are welcome to join our PRIVATE PILOT ground school—no flight training hours are required.

# Classes will start on September 9, Monday:

PRIVATE PILOT CLASSES EVERY TUESDAY AND THURSDAY: 12:00PM – 2:00PM  **OR**  6:00PM – 8:00PM.

INSTRUMENT CLASSES EVERY MONDAY AND WEDNESDAY: 12:00PM – 2:00PM  **OR**  6:00PM – 8:00PM.

# Why Choose Vegas Aviation Ground School?

- **Comprehensive Curriculum:** Stay ahead with our expanded course offerings, including the new Instrument Ground School.
- **Open Enrollment:** You don’t need to be a Vegas Aviation student to join. Our ground school is open to everyone!
- **Flexible Scheduling:** Our night classes are perfect for those with daytime commitments. Missed the day session? No problem. Join us later that evening.
